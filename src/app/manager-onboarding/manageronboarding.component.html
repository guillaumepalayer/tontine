Frequently asked questions

What is Kairo token?

Kairo is Betoken's non-transferable reputation token. Managers need to possess Kairo tokens to manage funds on Betoken. They represent how good a manager is at investing. The more Kairo tokens you have, the more money in the Betoken fund you can manage and the more income you‚Äôll get every month as commissions on your investments.

How can I buy Kairo tokens?

Click on the Get Started button and follow the instructions. You'll be able to use Ether or any of our 70+ supported ERC-20 tokens to buy Kairo tokens.

Do I receive my Kairo tokens immediately after purchase?

Yes. The Kairo tokens will be given to your wallet after your purchase transaction has been accepted by the Ethereum blockchain, which may take up to a few minutes.

What is the commissions structure?

Management commissions

After the management phase is over (every 27 days):

IF the fund is profitable OR not profitable

0.1% of the total Betoken funds is set aside and distributed among fund manager holding Kairo tokens.

ONLY IF the fund is profitable

20% of the total Betoken's monthly profits is set aside as commission and distributed among fund managers holding Kairo tokens proportional to the amount they hold and their risk profile. The commission is taken into account IF and ONLY IF the fund is profitable during the actual investment cycle.

How can I manage and follow the activity of the fund?

You have access to a Portal where you can view Betoken's fund performances and your manager trading interface.

Can I sell my Kairo tokens?

No. Kairo is a non-transferable reputation token, which means that you cannot sell or give your Kairo tokens to someone else.

Can I buy more Kairo tokens after with the same Wallet address?

No. You can only buy Kairo tokens once per Ethereum account.
